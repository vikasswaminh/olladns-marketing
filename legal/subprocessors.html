What we don't use

To save you from asking: we do not run customer data through any analytics SDK (Mixpanel, Segment, Amplitude, Pendo, FullStory, Hotjar, Posthog), CRM enrichment service (Clearbit, ZoomInfo), advertising platform (Google, Meta, LinkedIn pixels), or data broker. We are not on Google Tag Manager. We do not use ML model training APIs on customer DNS data.

Subprocessor change notice

When we add, remove, or change a subprocessor, we'll email everyone subscribed to subprocessors@olladns.com at least 30 days before the change takes effect. You can object to a change via that address; if we can't move forward without the new subprocessor and you don't accept, we'll let you terminate without penalty and refund prepaid time.

Sub-subprocessors

Several of our subprocessors use their own subprocessors (e.g. Cloudflare uses AWS for some functions; Stripe uses many regional acquirers). We rely on their respective DPAs and notification practices for those sub-tiers rather than reproducing them here. If you need a specific sub-subprocessor inventory for compliance purposes, email legal@olladns.com.

Data Processing Agreement

Our DPA (Standard Contractual Clauses for EU transfers, GDPR Art. 28 obligations, DPDP §11 obligations) is countersigned in your order form for enterprise plans, or auto-included when you accept the Terms of Service on self-serve plans. A standalone signed DPA is available for free on request from legal@olladns.com.
