Trust & Security

We host the most sensitive layer of your network. Here’s how we earn it.

SOC 2 Type II

Observation period in progress. Report expected Q3 2026 under NDA. Until then, our posture page has the unvarnished current state.

ISO 27001

Not yet certified. On the roadmap after SOC 2 lands. We don't claim it elsewhere on the site — if you see it, that's a copy bug; tell us.

HIPAA BAA

Not offered today. We only see DNS query names (not payloads), and per-tenant client-IP anonymization is real — but a signed BAA isn't on the table yet. Tell us if it's a hard requirement.

Encryption in transit

DoH, DoT, and DoQ on every resolver. TLS 1.3 minimum, Let's Encrypt cert rotation.

Data residency

Single US region today. EU and APAC regions on the multi-region roadmap; until they ship, all data lives in our US LXC.

Vulnerability reporting

No formal bug-bounty program yet. Reports welcome via security-policy.html — we acknowledge within one business day. Paid program lands after SOC 2.
