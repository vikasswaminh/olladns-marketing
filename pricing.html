FAQ

Common questions.

Why per-tenant flat pricing instead of per-device or per-query?

Per-query bills punish you for getting useful (more devices = more queries = bigger bill). Per-device bills punish you for protecting more people. Per-tenant flat pricing lets you scale your usage without surprise overages. Soft caps exist for fair-use enforcement; we warn before you'd be billed extra.

What happens at the soft cap?

We email at 80% and 100%. Going over is fine for a month; we ask you to upgrade if it persists. We never auto-bill for overage without your written agreement — no surprises.

Why are MCP tool calls metered separately from REST?

An AI agent driving the MCP server can fan-out to dozens of tool calls per user request — listing tenants, fetching policies, scoring detections, applying changes. That's the value, but it's also a different cost profile than a single REST call from a script. We meter them separately so you can size your plan against the agentic workload you actually have. The Pro tier's 10K MCP calls/mo comfortably covers daily use by 2–3 engineers driving Claude / Cursor; Business at 250K covers a SOC of ~20 routinely automating triage.

Free tier limitations are real?

Yes. Free is for evaluation and hobby use. It excludes MCP, webhooks, and scoped API keys because those are commercial-tier features. The 100K queries/mo limit is enough for a handful of devices in a single home; not enough for a team.

Can I downgrade?

Yes, instantly, at the next billing cycle. Your data is retained through the downgrade — but features locked behind higher tiers (extended retention, additional API keys, etc.) snap to the lower tier's limits. Nothing is deleted; you just lose access to the features.

Is there an India / DPDP-specific plan?

Pricing is the same in INR (with GST). DPDP-specific data-residency requires the Mumbai region which is on the Enterprise roadmap. Until then, Indian customers run on our US region — we acknowledge this is a gap and are happy to confirm timeline in writing.

What about MSP / reseller pricing?

Talk to us. Volume pricing for 10+ customer tenants under one billing relationship is real and negotiated case-by-case. White-label (you front-end your customers; we provide the backend) is a separate conversation — we don't ship a generic white-label product today.

Can I self-host?

The full stack is in a GitHub repo — postgres, clickhouse, AdGuard Home, the Rust API, the FastMCP sidecar, all in one docker-compose. License terms for production self-hosting are a paid Enterprise conversation; eval/internal use under fair-use terms is fine.
