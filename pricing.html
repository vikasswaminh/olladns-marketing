Pricing FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What counts as a “device”?

Any endpoint that resolves DNS through olladns — a laptop, phone, tablet, server, kiosk, or IoT device. On-network devices behind a site (office Wi-Fi) are counted by their unique resolved client over a rolling 30-day window. We do not double-count a laptop that uses the roaming client and also visits the office.

Is there really a free trial?

Yes. 14 days, no credit card, full feature access up to 250 devices. We just ask for a work email and your company name so our team can offer help if you want it. At the end you can pay, request an extension, or walk away.

Do I have to commit for a year?

Annual is our default and saves you ~18%. Month-to-month is available on Business at a higher rate. Enterprise contracts are typically annual or multi-year for the committed-volume discount.

How is query data priced?

Query volume is unmetered on every plan. You pay per device — not per query, byte, or rule. We’ve been burned by metered SaaS too.

Do you have non-profit / education / startup pricing?

Yes — 25% off Business for registered 501(c)(3) non-profits, public schools, and accredited universities. Startups under 3 years old and 25 employees can get six months free; apply here.

What if my device count fluctuates?

You commit to a baseline. Going under has no effect (you pay the baseline). Going over auto-meters at a published per-device overage rate at the end of the month. We never auto-upgrade your plan tier.

Can I get a security questionnaire / SOC 2 report?

Yes. SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001:2022, our pentest summary, and a pre-filled CAIQ are all available under NDA via our Trust Center. HIPAA BAA is included with Enterprise; available on Business by request.
