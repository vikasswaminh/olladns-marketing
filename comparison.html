Where olladns isn't the right pick

  • You need global anycast PoPs today. olladns runs in one US region with a Mumbai region on the roadmap. If sub-20 ms p99 from Sydney matters more than agent-native config, NextDNS or ControlD win on infrastructure today.
  • You need a packaged router agent. Both NextDNS and ControlD ship CLIs that drop into OpenWrt / pfSense / Asus-Merlin / etc. We don't — your router needs to do DoH/DoT upstream natively, or you front it with your own forwarder.
  • You need native iOS / Android apps. Out of scope for olladns; configure via OS-native DoH or a third-party DNS-changer app.
  • You need a vendor name a Fortune-500 CISO recognizes. NextDNS and ControlD are 4+ years older. SOC 2 in progress here; until that lands, large-enterprise procurement teams will need a leap of faith.
