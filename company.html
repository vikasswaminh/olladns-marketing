How we work

Six things we believe.

01

The query log is the source of truth.

Dashboards can lie. Numbers can be rounded. The raw record of what was asked, when, by whom, and what the verdict was — that’s where the actual security work happens.

02

False positives are the enemy.

Every wrong block is a helpdesk ticket and a step toward analysts disabling rules. We measure FPs as a first-class metric and tune for them obsessively.

03

Speed is a feature.

If our resolver adds more than ~5ms to a lookup, people notice — and they shouldn’t. We measure p50, p99, and p99.9 latency every week, in every region.

04

Default to honest.

No marketing math, no “up to” statistics, no benchmark cherry-picking. If we don’t lead on something, we’ll tell you the truth and try to fix it.

05

Build for the SOC.

Designed by people who’ve been woken up at 3am. Every screen, every alert, every drawer is shaped by that experience — not by a focus group.

06

Ship the unsexy.

Audit logs, role-based access, RBAC migration tools, IPv6 support — the boring features that earn the boring trust that makes the rest possible.

History

Four years, two oceans, one stubborn idea.

2021

Founded in a Brooklyn brownstone

Three engineers — two from a fintech SOC, one from a CDN’s resolver team — agreed that DNS-layer security needed a serious rebuild. They drafted the initial architecture on the back of a Manhattan-bound G-train.

2022

First customer · Seed round

Northwind Labs went live in February. By November, 60 customers; $4M seed from Wing VC, Heavybit, and a small angel syndicate of security CTOs.

2023

AI Detection · Berlin office

First production ML model — phishing lookalike, v1. Opened a Berlin engineering office for threat research, anchored by two former Mandiant analysts.

2024

Series B · 43 POPs · 1,000 customers

$74M Series B led by Index Ventures. Rolled out 43-POP anycast network. Passed the 1,000-customer mark. Shipped the MSP console, opened the public bug bounty.

2025

4,000+ customers · ISO 27001

Crossed 4,000 customers across 62 countries. Achieved ISO 27001 certification. Shipped Terraform provider v1, Sentinel native connector, and the v4 model series.

2026

The next chapter

FedRAMP Moderate in progress. APAC research lab opening Q3. New product surfaces around identity-bound policy and zero-trust DNS that we’re excited to talk about soon.
